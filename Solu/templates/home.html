Why do we need Virtue?


The degradation of almost a billion hectares of forest land worldwide has affected about 3.2 billion people and led to the extinction of various species while intensifying the effects of climate change.


However, Data Science and Machine Learning offer a potential solution to this problem by enabling the identification of diseases and timely implementation of appropriate measures. This can help to sustainably manage forests, combat desertification, reverse land degradation, and halt the loss of biodiversity.


The project aims to help our target customers, who are forest and grove conservationists over large areas. Currently, disease infestation from pests, fungus, etc. is a serious problem that needs to be eliminated as soon as possible. Checking for fungal infections in vast farmlands can be very time-consuming and requires a lot of manual labor.
